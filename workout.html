Full-Body Strength Training

Boost your strength with exercises targeting every major muscle group.

  • Benefits:

  • Time-efficient: Works multiple muscle groups in one session
  • overall strength: Engages major muscle groups, promoting balanced development.
  • Boosts metabolism: Increases calorie burn during and after workouts.
  • Versatile: Suitable for beginners to advanced lifters, can be done with bodyweight, free weights, or machines.

Full-Body Strength Training Routine:

  • Dynamic stretches: Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists.
  • Light cardio: Jumping jacks, brisk walking, or cycling to increase heart rate.
  • Workout Structure Frequency: Aim for 2-3 times per week, with at least one rest day in between.

HIIT Workout

High-intensity workout to boost your metabolism and burn calories.

  • Benefits:

  • Time-efficient: You can achieve significant results in 20-30 minutes.
  • Increased calorie burn: HIIT can lead to higher calorie expenditure during and after the workout due to the afterburn effect (EPOC).
  • Improved cardiovascular health: Enhances heart and lung capacity.
  • Versatility: Can be done with bodyweight, weights, or cardio equipment.

HIIT Workout Routine:

HIIT Workout routines include Jumping jacks, Arm circles, Bodyweight squats, Burpees, High knees and push ups.

Cardio & Core

Burn calories and strengthen your core with these effective exercises.

  • Benefits:

  • Improved Cardiovascular Health: Engaging in regular cardio strengthens the heart, increases blood circulation, and improves overall cardiovascular endurance.
  • Enhanced Core Strength: Core exercises build stability, balance, and strength in the abdominal and lower back muscles, which are crucial for overall movement and posture.
  • Improved Posture: Strengthening the core can lead to better posture, which reduces strain on the back and neck.
  • Stress Relief:Both cardio and core workouts release endorphins, which can help reduce stress and improve mood.

cardio Circuit

It includes Jumping jacks, High knees, Butt kicks,Russian twists,Bicycle crunches and leg raises.
